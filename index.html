Privacy Policy for Mozhimathi

Mozhimathi is an intelligent subtitle generation and translation application designed to make video content more accessible by providing automated subtitle generation for Tanglish (Tamil-English mixed) content with multi-language output support. Your privacy is important to us.

1. Data Collection and Storage

Your video uploads, subtitle preferences, and processing history are securely stored using Firebase services (RealtimeDatabase and Firebase Storage). We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal data. Your data is not shared with any unauthorized parties.

2. Video Processing

Videos uploaded to Mozhimathi are processed using advanced AI models including:

  • Whisper model for English speech transcription
  • Google Translate services for multi-language translation
  • Speech recognition libraries for Tanglish processing

Processed videos and generated subtitles are stored securely and are only accessible to you through your authenticated account.

3. Backend Infrastructure

Mozhimathi utilizes Google Cloud Platform (GCP) services to host our backend servers that handle all AI processing operations. Our backend infrastructure includes:

  • GCP Compute Engine: Hosts our main application servers that process video uploads and coordinate subtitle generation
  • AI Model Processing: All Whisper model operations and speech recognition tasks are performed on our GCP-hosted backend servers
  • Secure Communication: All data transmission between the mobile app and our GCP backend servers is encrypted using industry-standard protocols

This backend infrastructure ensures reliable, scalable, and secure processing of your videos while maintaining high performance and data protection standards.

4. Permissions

Mozhimathi may request the following permissions:

  • Storage Access: To access and upload video files from your device
  • Download Folder Access: To save generated subtitle files (SRT/VTT formats) to your device's download folder for easy access and sharing
  • Internet Access: For communication with our backend servers and cloud services

These permissions are used solely for the app's core functionality of subtitle generation, translation, and file management. Download folder access is specifically required to provide you with the generated subtitle files on your device.

5. Authentication

We use Firebase Authentication with Gmail provider for secure user authentication. No passwords are stored on our servers, and authentication is handled through Google's secure infrastructure.

6. No Third-Party Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data, videos, or generated subtitles with any third-party services beyond the essential services (Firebase for storage, Google Cloud Platform for backend processing, Google Translate for translation) required for app functionality.

7. Data Retention

Your videos and subtitles are retained in your account until you choose to delete them. You have full control over your data and can request deletion at any time.
